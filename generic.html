Parental Leave in The United States

The case of study is about paternity leave trends and patterns among the different sectors. The main conclusion is that actual equality between mothers and fathers has not yet been achieved. First, most companies do no offer paid paternity leave, and second when they do, there are still large gaps between maternity and paternity leaves. Therefore, when hiring, there is a remaining bias depending on gender, since gender has an economic impact on the company. #mavenfamilyleavechallenge

Northwind Traders KPIS

Northwind Traders is a fictitious gourmet food supplier that provided datasets containing information on customers, products, orders, shippers, and employees. The main objective of the project was to gain quick insights into the company's performance in key areas. Here are the key highlights of the project: sales trend, product performance, key customers, shipping costs, takeaways and some recommendations. Based on the insights gained from the dataset, the project provided valuable takeaways and recommendations. These recommendations suggested areas where the company's performance was strong and highlighted opportunities for further analysis. This could involve strategies to increase sales or reduce excessive costs. During the project I used calculation on Tableau to find insights and to gain a better understand and create some KPIs> By using visualization on Tableau, I was able to identify areas where the company succeeded and areas that required further analysis to improve sales or reduce costs.( The dataset are provide from Maven Analytics #mavenchallenge).
